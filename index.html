Akan Name WebApp.

The app takes the users date of birth and gender via the HTML form below. It then calculates the weekday and gives the user his or her Akan name depending on there gender. Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. Here are ghanian day names.

Male Names.

  • Sunday: Kwasi
  • Monday: Kwadwo
  • Tuesday: Kwabena
  • Wednesday: Kwaku
  • Thursday: Yaw
  • Friday: Kofi
  • Saturday: Kwame

Female Names.

  • Sunday: Akosua
  • Monday: Adwoa
  • Tuesday: Abenaa
  • Wednesday: Akua
  • Thursday: Yaa
  • Friday: Afua
  • Saturday: Ama

Try filling in your birth date and gender in the form below to know your Akan Name. Have fun by filling in some of your friends birthdays also.
